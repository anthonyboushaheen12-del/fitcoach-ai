# Supabase Email Setup

## Disable Email Confirmation (recommended for development)

If you're not receiving confirmation emails or want instant sign-up:

1. Open [Supabase Dashboard](https://supabase.com/dashboard) → Your project
2. Go to **Authentication** → **Providers** → **Email**
3. Turn **OFF** "Confirm email"
4. Click **Save**

New users will be confirmed immediately without an email.

---

## Resend Verification Email

If you keep email confirmation enabled, users who don't receive the email can click **"Resend verification email"** on the sign-up screen after creating their account.
